About Me

My name is Francois du Toit, the Tall Guy Coder, a new and enthusiastic developer who is passionate about creating innovative solutions to complex problems. With a strong foundation in web development and a hunger to learn more, I am always seeking new challenges to expand my skill set. My attention to detail and dedication to writing clean, efficient code makes me a valuable asset to any team. I am excited to bring my unique perspective and creativity to the world of web development.

More about me...

Hello world! My name is Francois du Toit. I am currently an educator based in Cape Town working for the Department of Education.
I am known for my "tallness" - hence the name of 'Tall Guy' Coder - and also for the continuous smiles and hard work I bring to the table.
Ever since I can remember, I've been infront of a computer, obsessing over figuring out how the software or hardware works. Ive always been interested in a career in tech, but always thought I was not smart enough to follow through.
In September 2022, my baby boy was born. This sparked something in me. I decided then and there that I wanted a better life for my family.
"When words become unclear, I shall focus with photographs. When images become inadequate, I shall be content with silence." - Ansel Adams.
I've recently been made the luckiest man alive when I married the girl of my dreams. And in truth, she is the backbone of this business and the reason it exists today. Without her push I will not be standing tall today!
Thank you so much for showing an interest in my work! It's an absolute pleasure for me to work with you to create something beautiful for your business.
